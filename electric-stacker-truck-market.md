<p><ul><li>Counterbalance</li><li>Warehouse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric stacker truck market consists of two main types: counterbalance and warehouse. Counterbalance electric stacker trucks are designed with a weight at the back to counterbalance the weight being lifted, allowing them to operate in narrow spaces. They are commonly used in outdoor areas or loading docks. Warehouse electric stacker trucks, on the other hand, are specifically designed for indoor use in narrow aisles and tight spaces. They are ideal for efficiently moving and stacking pallets in warehouses or distribution centers.</p></p>
