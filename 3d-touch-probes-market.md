<p><p>3D touch probes are devices used for precision measurement in manufacturing processes. Hard-wired probes are connected to the measuring machine through physical wires, ensuring a reliable and consistent connection. Radio probes use wireless technology to transmit measurement data to the machine, allowing for greater flexibility and mobility. Optical probes rely on optical sensors to measure distances and displacements, offering high accuracy and non-contact measurement capabilities. These different market types cater to various needs and preferences in the industry, providing options for different applications and user requirements.</p></p>
