<p><p>Powertrain refers to the group of components in a vehicle that converts stored energy into motion. These factors are driving the development and adoption of advanced powertrain technologies.</p><p>The emergence of electric powertrains is reshaping the powertrain market. The demand for electric vehicles (EVs) is increasing rapidly due to factors like government subsidies, technological advancements, and environmental concerns. Manufacturers are investing heavily in electric powertrain development to meet the surging demand for EVs.</p><p>The market for internal combustion engine (ICE) powertrains is also evolving. Industry players are focusing on developing advanced ICE powertrains to comply with stricter emission norms. This has led to the implementation of technologies like downsizing, turbocharging, and direct fuel injection, which improve the performance and efficiency of ICE powertrains.</p><p>The powertrain market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for electric and hybrid vehicles, along with advancements in powertrain technologies, is driving market growth. The market for electric powertrains is expected to grow significantly as more countries and governments focus on reducing emissions and promoting electric mobility.</p><p>However, the high cost of electric powertrain components and the limited charging infrastructure pose challenges to the market's growth.
