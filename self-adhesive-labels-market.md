<p><p>Self adhesive labels are widely used in various industries for packaging and labeling purposes. The market for self adhesive labels is classified into different types based on the printing technology used. Offset print involves transferring ink from a plate to a rubber blanket before printing on the label, while flexography print utilizes flexible relief plates. Rotogravure print involves etching the design onto a cylinder. Screen print uses a stencil to transfer ink onto the label, and letterpress print applies ink directly onto the label. Lastly, digital print uses digital files to directly print the label. These various printing types provide different benefits and suitability for specific labeling needs.</p></p>
