<p><p>A Transcutaneous Oximetry System is a non-invasive medical device used to measure the oxygen saturation level in the blood through the skin. It consists of a sensor that is placed on the skin, which then transmits the oxygen saturation readings to a monitor. This technology is used in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, and home care, to assess and monitor oxygenation in patients with conditions such as respiratory disorders, wounds, or cardiovascular diseases.</p><p>The future outlook of the Transcutaneous Oximetry System market appears promising.
