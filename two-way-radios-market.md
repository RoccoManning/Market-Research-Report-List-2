<p><p>Two-way radios are used for communication and are available in two main types: analog and digital. Analog radios use continuous waves to transmit voice signals, converting sound into electrical signals and transmitting them through radio waves. They are more susceptible to interference and have limited features. On the other hand, digital radios convert sound into binary code, offering clearer audio, increased range, and additional functionalities like text messaging and GPS tracking. Digital radios also have enhanced security features, making them more reliable for critical communication needs.</p></p>
