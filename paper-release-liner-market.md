<p><p>A paper release liner is a type of material used in various industries as a backing for labels, tapes, and other adhesive products. Advancements in coating technologies have significantly improved the performance of paper release liners, making them more durable and efficient. These liners ensure the safety and integrity of the adhesive products, meeting the required industry standards.</p><p>Overall, the paper release liner market is poised for healthy growth in the coming years.
