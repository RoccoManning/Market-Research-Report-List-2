<p><p>Area CCD image sensors are commonly used in digital cameras and other imaging devices. There are two types of area CCD image sensors: Interline Transfer and Frame Line Transfer. </p><p>Interline Transfer sensors use a special structure where each photosensitive pixel is paired with a separate storage element, allowing for faster readout speed and higher pixel density. On the other hand, Frame Line Transfer sensors have an additional line to transfer photocurrents to the output amplifier, resulting in improved sensitivity, dynamic range, and signal-to-noise ratio. These different types cater to specific requirements in various applications, providing enhanced performance for different imaging needs.</p></p>
