<p><p>The Pressure Sensitive Adhesives (PSAs) market for Flexible PCBs can be categorized into four types. Thermosetting adhesives are heat-curable and provide strong bonding. Liquid adhesives are applied as a liquid and then cured to form a bond. Thermoplastic adhesives soften when heated and solidify when cooled, allowing for multiple re-workability. UV curable adhesives are cured using ultraviolet light exposure, providing quick and efficient bonding. These different types of adhesives offer various benefits and are used based on specific requirements in the Flexible PCB industry.</p></p>
