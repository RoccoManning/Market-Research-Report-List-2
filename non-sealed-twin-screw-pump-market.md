<p><p>The non-sealed twin-screw pump market consists of two main types: single volute twin screw pump and double volutes twin screw pump. </p><p>A single volute twin screw pump has a single volute casing, which means that the fluid enters and exits the pump through the same opening. On the other hand, a double volutes twin screw pump has two separate volutes, allowing for more efficient fluid flow and reduced leakage. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications for reliable and efficient pumping of liquids.</p></p>
