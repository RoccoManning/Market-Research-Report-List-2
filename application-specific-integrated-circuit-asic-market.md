<p><strong>The Application-specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Standard-cell Based</li><li>Gate-array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) market can be categorized into three types based on the design methodology. Full-custom design involves creating a chip from scratch, allowing high performance and low power consumption but with high development costs. Standard-cell-based ASICs use pre-designed cells for faster development and lower costs but compromise on performance. Gate-array-based ASICs utilize pre-fabricated gates that can be customized as per requirements, offering moderate performance, lower development costs, and shorter time-to-market. Each market type suits different applications and cost considerations, enabling manufacturers to choose an appropriate design methodology for their specific needs.</p></p>
