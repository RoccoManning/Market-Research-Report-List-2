<p><p>A compression plate is a medical device used in orthopedic surgeries to fix fractures in bones. It is a metal plate designed to be placed on the surface of a fractured bone and secured with screws. The compression plate provides stability to the bone, allowing it to heal properly. It helps in maintaining alignment and promoting bone growth.</p><p>The global compression plate market is projected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing prevalence of bone fractures due to sports injuries, accidents, and a growing geriatric population is driving the market. Additionally, advancements in surgical techniques and the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>The market is also benefiting from the development of innovative compression plate designs and materials, such as titanium and stainless steel, which offer improved biocompatibility and reduce the risk of complications.
