<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems come in three main types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS). CEPS uses an electric motor located on the steering column to assist with steering, while PEPS utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering pinion. REPS, on the other hand, employs an electric motor mounted on the steering rack. All three systems provide electronic assistance to the steering mechanism, improving maneuverability and reducing the effort required by the driver.</p></p>
