<p><p>The dual chamber vacuum heat treatment furnace market includes two main types: horizontal type and vertical type. The horizontal type furnace is designed with a horizontally oriented chamber where the heat treatment process takes place. It is commonly used for larger and longer parts that require uniform heating. On the other hand, the vertical type furnace has a vertically oriented chamber, and it is suitable for smaller and shorter parts. Both types of furnaces offer efficient heat treatment processes in a vacuum environment, catering to different sizes and shapes of components.</p></p>
