<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The dental fiberglass posts market consists of three main types: tapered, parallel, and other. Tapered posts have a conical shape, gradually diminishing in size from the coronal to apical end. They are commonly used in cases of minimal tooth structure remaining. Parallel posts have a consistent diameter throughout their length and are suitable for teeth with adequate tooth structure. The "other" category includes various types of posts that do not fit into the tapered or parallel groups, such as serrated or serrated-tapered posts. Each type has its own specific applications and benefits within the dental field.</p></p>
