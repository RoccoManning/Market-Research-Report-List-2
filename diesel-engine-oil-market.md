<p><p>Diesel engine oil, also known as diesel fuel lubricants or diesel motor oil, is a type of lubricant specifically developed to meet the needs and requirements of diesel engines. The growing demand for diesel vehicles, particularly in emerging economies where transportation and industrial sectors are expanding, is one of the key factors driving the market.
