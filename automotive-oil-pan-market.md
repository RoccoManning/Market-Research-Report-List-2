<p><strong>The Automotive Oil Pan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structural Oil Pan</li><li>Non-Structural Oil Pan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive oil pan market consists of two main types: structural oil pans and non-structural oil pans. Structural oil pans are typically made of cast aluminum or steel and serve as an integral part of the engine's structure, providing support and rigidity. On the other hand, non-structural oil pans are often made of stamped steel or plastic and are detachable components. They are used mainly for containing and storing oil within the engine, without contributing to the structural integrity of the vehicle.</p></p>
