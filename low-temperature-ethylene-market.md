<p><p>The low temperature ethylene market can be classified into two types: polymer grade and chemical grade. Polymer grade ethylene is mainly used for the production of various types of polymers such as polyethylene. It requires high purity levels and is mostly obtained through steam cracking of hydrocarbons. Chemical grade ethylene, on the other hand, is primarily used as a starting material for the production of various chemicals and solvents. The purity requirements are relatively lower compared to polymer grade ethylene, and it is usually obtained through the refining process. Both types play a crucial role in different industries.</p></p>
