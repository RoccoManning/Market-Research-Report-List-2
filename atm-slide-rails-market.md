<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The ATM slide rails market can be classified into three types based on the extension capabilities of the rails: full extension, partial extension, and over extension. Full extension slide rails allow the drawer to be fully extended outside the ATM for easy access and maintenance. Partial extension slide rails enable the drawer to be partially extended, providing limited access without completely removing it. Over extension slide rails offer maximum extension capability, allowing the drawer to be fully pulled out of the ATM, facilitating convenient loading and unloading of cash or other items.</p></p>
