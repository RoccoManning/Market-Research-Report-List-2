<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are flat boards made of non-conductive materials such as fiberglass, laminates, or ceramics with conductive tracks or pathways etched onto them. Factors such as the growing adoption of IoT devices, the emergence of 5G technology, and the rapid digitalization in developing countries are driving the demand for advanced PCBs. Additionally, the miniaturization trend in electronic devices is also likely to fuel the market growth as smaller and more efficient PCBs are required.</p><p>Moreover, the automotive industry's shift towards electric vehicles and the deployment of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) are expected to create significant growth opportunities for the PCB market. Single panel PCBs consist of only one layer of conductive material, while double panel PCBs have two layers. Four-layer and six-layer boards provide additional layers of copper conductors, enhancing circuit complexity.
