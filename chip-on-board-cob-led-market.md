<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market is categorized into three types: Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip. Lateral Chip refers to the LED chip placed horizontally on the circuit board, providing broader light distribution. Vertical Chip involves placing the LED chip vertically onto the board, allowing for better heat dissipation. Flip Chip technology involves mounting the LED chip upside-down, eliminating wire bonds and improving thermal management. These different market types cater to varied requirements of lighting applications, providing options for light distribution, heat dissipation, and size constraints.</p></p>
