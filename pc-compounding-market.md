<p><p>PC Compounding is the process of combining polycarbonate (PC) with other materials to enhance its properties and applications. PC/ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene), PC/PBT (polybutylene terephthalate), PC/ASA (acrylic styrene acrylonitrile), PC/PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate), and PC/PET (polyethylene terephthalate) are some common combinations. PC/ABS offers good impact resistance and heat resistance. PC/PBT provides excellent mechanical strength and resistance to chemicals. PC/ASA combines good impact resistance with UV stability. PC/PMMA offers improved transparency and weatherability. PC/PET provides high heat resistance and dimensional stability. These combinations expand the range of properties and applications of polycarbonate.</p></p>
