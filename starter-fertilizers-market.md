<p><p>Starter fertilizers are categorized based on the types of nutrients they provide to plants. Nitrogen fertilizers are composed mainly of nitrogen and promote leafy growth. Phosphorus fertilizers contain phosphorus, which aids in root development and flower/fruit production. Potassium fertilizers provide plants with potassium, supporting overall plant health and disease resistance. Micronutrient fertilizers consist of trace elements like iron, zinc, and manganese, which are essential for various metabolic functions in plants. These different types of starter fertilizers cater to specific nutrient needs of plants and contribute to their overall growth and productivity.</p></p>
