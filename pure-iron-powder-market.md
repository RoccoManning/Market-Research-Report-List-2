<p><p>The pure iron powder market is comprised of two major types: carbonyl iron powder and electrolytic iron powder. Carbonyl iron powder is produced by thermal decomposition of iron pentacarbonyl, resulting in a fine and spherical powder. It is widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and metallurgy due to its excellent magnetic properties. On the other hand, electrolytic iron powder is produced through an electrolysis process, resulting in irregular-shaped particles. It finds applications in sectors such as pharmaceuticals, food fortification, and chemical industries.</p></p>
