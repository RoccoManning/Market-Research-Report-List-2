<p><p>The high-duty refractory material market can be categorized into two types: shaped high-duty refractory and unshaped high-duty refractory. Shaped high-duty refractory materials refer to those that are pre-formed into specific shapes and sizes such as bricks, blocks, and tiles. On the other hand, unshaped high-duty refractory materials are not pre-formed and can be applied directly in a wet or dry form. Both types are used in various industries for their excellent thermal insulation, chemical resistance, and mechanical strength properties.</p></p>
