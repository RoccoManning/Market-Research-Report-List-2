<p><p>The bidet market encompasses three main types: conventional bidets, bidet showers, and add-on bidets. Conventional bidets are standalone fixtures separate from the toilet, featuring a water spray nozzle for personal hygiene. Bidet showers, also known as handheld bidets or shattafs, are attached to the existing toilet and have a hose-like structure with a spray nozzle. Add-on bidets are installed on top of the existing toilet seat and provide a water spray function. These market types cater to different preferences and requirements for personal hygiene, offering various options for individuals seeking bidet solutions.</p></p>
