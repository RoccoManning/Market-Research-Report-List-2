<p><p>Dust concentration detectors are used to measure the amount of dust particles present in air or gas. There are three types of detectors commonly used in the market. Capacitance measurement involves measuring the change in capacitance when dust particles pass through an electric field. Beta ray measurement uses the absorption of beta radiation by dust particles to determine their concentration. Light absorption measurement utilizes the absorption of light by dust particles to estimate their concentration. These detectors provide important data for maintaining air quality in various industries.</p></p>
