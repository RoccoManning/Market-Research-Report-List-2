<p><strong>The Architectural Window Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solar Control Film</li><li>Safety/Security Film</li><li>Decorative Film</li><li>Spectrally Selective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural window film includes four major types: solar control film, safety/security film, decorative film, and spectrally selective film. Solar control film is designed to reduce heat and glare from the sun, while maintaining natural light transmission. Safety/security film provides protection against break-ins, accidents, and natural disasters by holding shattered glass together. Decorative film offers various patterns and designs for enhancing privacy and aesthetics. Spectrally selective film allows visible light to pass through while blocking heat-causing infrared and ultraviolet rays. These films contribute to energy efficiency, comfort, safety, and personalized interior design in buildings.</p></p>
