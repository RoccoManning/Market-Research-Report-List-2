<p><p>Laser microscopes are classified based on the wavelength of the laser used. In the market, there are three types of laser microscopes based on laser wavelength: less than 400nm, 400-600nm, and more than 600nm. Laser wavelength less than 400nm microscopes are used for high-resolution imaging and analysis of nanoscale structures. Laser wavelength 400-600nm microscopes provide a balance between resolution and penetration depth for various biomedical applications. Laser wavelength more than 600nm microscopes are suitable for deep tissue imaging due to their higher penetration capabilities.</p></p>
