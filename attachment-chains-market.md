<p><strong>The Attachment Chains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Pitch Attachment Chains</li><li>Double-Pitch Attachment Chains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Attachment chains are commonly used in various industries for conveying materials or transmitting power. Single-pitch attachment chains have attachments placed on every pitch link, allowing for precise positioning of loads. They are suitable for applications requiring accurate indexing. On the other hand, double-pitch attachment chains have attachments placed on every other pitch link, making them more economically viable when positioning is less critical. They are typically used for conveying lightweight materials or in applications where slower speeds are involved. Both types offer reliable performance and versatility in their respective applications.</p></p>
