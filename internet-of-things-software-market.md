<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based IoT software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage their IoT devices and data through the internet. On-premises IoT software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the user's hardware and network, allowing them to manage their IoT infrastructure entirely within their own premises.
