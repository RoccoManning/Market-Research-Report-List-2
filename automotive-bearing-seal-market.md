<p><p>The automotive bearing seal market comprises various types of seals utilized in vehicles. Dust seals are designed to prevent dust and dirt particles from entering the bearing. Oil seals are intended to retain lubricants inside the bearing and block the entry of contaminants. Pack seals consist of a combination of oil and dust seals, providing enhanced protection.
