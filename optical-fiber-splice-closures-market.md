<p><p>Optical fiber splice closures are used to protect and organize fiber optic cables. Dome type splice closures are compact and provide excellent protection against harsh environmental conditions. They are suitable for aerial, duct, and direct burial installations. Horizontal type splice closures are typically larger and can accommodate a higher number of fibers. They are usually installed on walls or poles and are commonly used in outdoor applications. Both dome and horizontal type splice closures are essential in ensuring the reliability and longevity of fiber optic networks.</p></p>
