<p><p>Steering lock systems are security devices installed in vehicles to prevent theft. T-lock is a popular type that secures the steering wheel by fitting over it, providing a highly visible deterrent. Top hook lock is another type that fastens the steering wheel to the brake or clutch pedal, impairing its movement. Lastly, baseball lock immobilizes the brake pedal, inhibiting the vehicle from being driven away. These different types of steering lock systems provide various levels of protection against theft, ensuring the safety of the vehicle.</p></p>
