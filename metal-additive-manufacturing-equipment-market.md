<p><strong>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal additive manufacturing equipment market includes various types of technologies. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt metal powder layer by layer, forming a solid object. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) employs an electron beam instead of a laser to melt the metal powder, enabling the production of complex and large-scale components. Other types of metal additive manufacturing technologies include binder jetting, directed energy deposition, and material extrusion, each with unique advantages and applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
