<p><p>Effect pigments market can be classified into two types: natural mineral pigments and artificial mineral pigments. Natural mineral pigments are derived from naturally occurring minerals, such as mica, talc, and iron oxide. These pigments offer unique colors and effects due to the varying composition of minerals. On the other hand, artificial mineral pigments are synthesized in laboratories and offer more consistent colors and effects. Both types of pigments are widely used in various industries, including automotive, coatings, plastics, and cosmetics, for their aesthetic and functional properties.</p></p>
