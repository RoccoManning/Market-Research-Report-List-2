<p><p>The automatic plastic sorting machine market can be classified into two main types: chute-type sorting machines and belt-type sorting machines. Chute-type sorting machines utilize a vertical chute system that allows plastics to be sorted based on their weight and size. On the other hand, belt-type sorting machines employ a conveyor belt system, which enables the plastics to be sorted based on their color or other identifying features. Both types of machines provide efficient and accurate sorting capabilities for various plastic materials in industries that require plastic recycling or waste management.</p></p>
