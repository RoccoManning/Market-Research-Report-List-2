<p><p>Rotary airlock valves are used in various industries to control the flow of bulk materials. Drop-through rotary valves are designed for applications where the material passes through the valve directly, ensuring reliable sealing and preventing air leakage. On the other hand, blow-through rotary valves are suitable for handling abrasive materials and high-pressure systems, as they allow the material to be blown through the valve with the help of an air source. Both types of valves play a crucial role in maintaining efficient material handling processes in different industrial settings.</p></p>
