Hunting tooth automotive chain sprockets have offset teeth that reduce noise during operation. Skip tooth automotive chain sprockets have widely spaced teeth for smoother engagement. Gap tooth automotive chain sprockets have a tooth gap that prevents debris buildup. Draw bench automotive chain sprockets are designed for heavy-duty applications. Segmental rim automotive chain sprockets have removable rim segments for easy maintenance and replacement.</p></p>
