<p><p>USB WiFi adapters are available in different market types: 802.11n, 802.11ac, and 802.11ax. The 802.11n adapters offer standard wireless connectivity with speeds up to 300Mbps. The 802.11ac adapters provide faster speeds up to 1.3Gbps, allowing for smoother streaming and online gaming experiences. The latest version, 802.11ax, offers even higher speeds up to 10Gbps, improved network efficiency, and better performance in high-density areas. Each market type caters to different user needs, providing varying levels of wireless connectivity and performance.</p></p>
