<p><p>The military unmanned ground vehicle (UGV) market consists of two main types: wheeled type and crawler type. </p><p>Wheeled UGVs are equipped with wheels for movement on various terrains, allowing them to travel at high speeds and cover large distances efficiently. They are suitable for operations in less challenging environments and are used for tasks such as reconnaissance, surveillance, and logistics support.</p><p>Crawler UGVs, on the other hand, employ tracks or continuous belts for propulsion, enabling them to navigate through complex and rugged terrains with ease. These UGVs are more versatile and can be employed for missions that require heavy payload capacity, clearance of obstacles, or tactical purposes such as combat or explosive ordnance disposal.</p><p>Both types offer unique capabilities to the military, and their selection depends on the specific operational requirements and environmental conditions they will encounter.</p></p>
