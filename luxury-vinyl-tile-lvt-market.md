Loose-lay LVT is a floating floor that doesn't require adhesive, making it easy to install and remove. Dry-back LVT needs adhesive applied to the subfloor for installation. Stone Plastic Composite (SPC) LVT is highly durable and water-resistant. Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) LVT offers similar features as SPC but with added comfort underfoot. Lastly, there are other variations in the LVT market, each with their unique properties and installation methods.</p></p>
