<p><p>Aluminum roofing market comprises two main types: tiles and sheets. Aluminum roof tiles are individual pieces that interlock to form a protective layer on the roof. These tiles offer durability, resistance to impact and corrosion, and come in various designs. On the other hand, aluminum roof sheets are larger, continuous panels that cover the entire roof area. They are flexible, lightweight, and known for their exceptional strength and longevity. Both tiles and sheets are popular choices in the aluminum roofing market, providing different solutions for various roofing needs.</p></p>
