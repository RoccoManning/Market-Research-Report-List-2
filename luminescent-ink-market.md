<p><p>Luminescent ink, also known as glow-in-the-dark ink, is a type of ink that glows in the dark or under ultraviolet (UV) light. It is widely used in various applications such as security printing, packaging, textiles, and signage, among others. Luminescent ink can be incorporated into different printing processes like offset, flexography, gravure, and screen printing, providing versatility in its usage.</p><p>The luminescent ink market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the rising demand for secure packaging solutions is driving the adoption of luminescent ink for security printing purposes. The ink is used to incorporate covert features in packaging, which helps in counterfeiting prevention. Secondly, the growing prominence of luminescent ink in the textile industry is fueling market growth. It is used to create unique designs on fabrics and clothing, enhancing their visual appeal. Additionally, the increasing adoption of luminescent ink in the sign and display industry for advertising purposes is boosting market demand.</p><p>In recent years, there have been several trends in the luminescent ink market. One of the key trends is the development of eco-friendly and sustainable luminescent inks. Manufacturers are focusing on creating ink formulations that reduce environmental impact without compromising on performance. Another trend is the incorporation of luminescent ink in smart packaging. This involves the use of sensors and indicators that rely on the luminescent properties of the ink to provide real-time information about product quality or freshness.</p><p>Overall, the luminescent ink market shows promising growth prospects due to the increasing demand for security printing, textile applications, and innovative packaging solutions. The development of eco-friendly variants and their integration into smart packaging are expected to be key trends driving the market in the coming years.</p></p>
