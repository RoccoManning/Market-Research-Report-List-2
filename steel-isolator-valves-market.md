<p><p>The steel isolator valves market consists of various types of valves, including ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, globe valves, and other types of valves. Ball valves are round valves that use a rotating ball with a hole in it to control the flow of fluid. Butterfly valves have a rotating disc that regulates fluid flow. Gate valves feature a sliding gate to start or stop the flow of liquids or gases. Globe valves have a spherical body and a disc that moves up and down to control the flow. Other types of valves include check valves, diaphragm valves, and plug valves.</p></p>
