<p><p>A car lead-acid battery is a type of rechargeable battery that is commonly used in vehicles. The increasing production and sales of passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and electric vehicles have been driving the market growth. As the average lifespan of a lead-acid battery is around 3-5 years, consumers often need to replace their batteries, especially in regions with extreme weather conditions.
