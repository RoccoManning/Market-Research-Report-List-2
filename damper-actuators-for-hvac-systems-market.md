<p><ul><li>Spring Return Damper Actuators</li><li>Non-Spring Return Damper Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Damper actuators are essential components in HVAC systems. Spring return damper actuators utilize a spring mechanism to control the opening and closing of dampers, providing reliable performance and safety in case of power failure. On the other hand, non-spring return damper actuators employ electronic control signals without springs, delivering precise positioning and longer lifespan. These types of actuators differ in their mechanisms but both contribute to efficient airflow regulation, temperature control, and energy conservation in HVAC systems.</p></p>
