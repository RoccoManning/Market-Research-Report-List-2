<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The coated casing market consists of different types, namely surface casing, intermediate casing, production casing, and drilling casing. Surface casing is the first layer of casing that is installed during the drilling process to protect shallow aquifers and provide structural support. Intermediate casing is employed to protect against fluid influx and provide mechanical strength in deeper sections. Production casing comes next, enclosing the wellbore to prevent reservoir fluids from escaping and facilitating extraction. Lastly, drilling casing is crucial during the drilling process to maintain stability and prevent well collapse.</p></p>
