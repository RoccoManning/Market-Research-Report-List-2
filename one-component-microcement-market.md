<p><p>The one-component microcement market can be categorized into various types, including sulfate resistant clinker, white clinker, and others. Sulfate resistant clinker is a type of microcement that contains low percentages of tricalcium aluminate, providing it with a high resistance to sulfate attack. On the other hand, white clinker is a microcement produced using raw materials with low iron content, resulting in a white appearance. The "others" category encompasses different types of microcement that do not fall into the sulfate resistant or white clinker classifications.</p></p>
