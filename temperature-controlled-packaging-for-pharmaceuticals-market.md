<p><p>Temperature controlled packaging for pharmaceuticals refers to the packaging solutions specifically designed to maintain the required temperature range during the transportation of temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals. Active temperature controlled systems utilize external power sources to actively regulate and monitor temperature, ensuring precise temperature control. On the other hand, passive temperature controlled systems utilize insulation materials such as vacuum panels or phase change materials to create a thermal barrier and maintain the desired temperature without the need for external power sources. Both systems are essential for the safe and effective transportation of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
