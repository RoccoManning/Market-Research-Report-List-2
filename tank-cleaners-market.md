<p><p>Tank cleaners are products used to clean tanks, such as those used for storage or transportation of liquids. There are different types of tank cleaners available in the market. Neutral tank cleaners have a pH level close to 7, making them suitable for cleaning tanks that contain sensitive materials, as they don't react strongly with them. On the other hand, alkaline tank cleaners have a higher pH level and are used for cleaning tanks that contain oily or greasy substances, as they have strong cleaning properties. These different market types cater to the specific needs of tank cleaning based on the materials present in the tanks.</p></p>
