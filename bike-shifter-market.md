<p><p>The bike shifter market can be categorized into two types: mechanical shifting and electronic shifting. Mechanical shifting involves the use of cables to actuate the movement of the derailleurs, which in turn changes the gear on the bike. It is a traditional and reliable system that requires manual input from the rider. On the other hand, electronic shifting utilizes electronic components and motors to control the gear changes. It offers precise and effortless shifting, with the ability to customize shift patterns. Electronic shifting is seen as a modern and advanced technology in the bike industry.</p></p>
