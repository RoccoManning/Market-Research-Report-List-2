<p><p>Double Block and Bleed (DBB) ball valves are types of valves that offer positive isolation for critical applications. They consist of two seating surfaces that completely seal off the pipeline on both ends, providing double blockage. Additionally, there is a venting cavity between the seating surfaces, which allows for bleeding off any trapped pressure between the two blockages. This design ensures maximum safety and prevents leakage, making them ideal for applications in oil and gas, chemical, and petrochemical industries.</p><p>The future outlook for the Double Block and Bleed Ball Valves Market is promising.
