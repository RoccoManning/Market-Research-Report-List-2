<p><p>A Vehicle Semi-Anechoic Chamber (VSAC) is a specialized acoustic test facility used for measuring the acoustic characteristics and noise emissions of vehicles. The chamber is designed to minimize external noise interference by using sound-absorbing materials to create a semi-anechoic environment.</p><p>The future outlook for the VSAC market is promising, driven by the increasing demand for quieter and more comfortable vehicles.
