<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) resins are widely used in the production of medical instruments due to their exceptional properties. There are two main types of POM resins available in the market: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene refers to POM resins made from a single monomer, resulting in a more crystalline and rigid material that offers excellent mechanical strength and dimensional stability. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene is produced by incorporating different monomers, leading to a more flexible and impact-resistant material suitable for applications where toughness is crucial.</p></p>
