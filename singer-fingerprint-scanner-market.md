<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Scanner</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The singer fingerprint scanner market consists of two main types: capacitive fingerprint scanners and optical fingerprint scanners. Capacitive scanners use small electrical currents to detect the ridges and valleys on a person's fingertip, capturing a high-resolution image for identification. On the other hand, optical scanners illuminate the fingerprint surface and capture the image using a camera. Both types are commonly used in various applications such as personal devices, access control systems, and attendance management systems for their accuracy and convenience in identifying individuals.</p></p>
