<p><p>The automotive metal forming market consists of various types of forming processes used in the manufacturing of automotive components. Roll forming involves shaping metal sheets into desired profiles through a series of rolls. Stretch forming stretches and shapes metal sheets over dies. Stamping uses dies to cut, shape, and form metal sheets into desired shapes. Deep drawing involves forming metal sheets into deeper shapes through a series of dies. Hydroforming uses high-pressure fluid to shape metal sheets into complex shapes. Other forms of metal forming include spinning, forging, and laser cutting.</p></p>
