<p><strong>The Truck Hydraulic Retarder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Tandem Hydraulic Retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck hydraulic retarder market is categorized into two types: parallel hydraulic retarder and tandem hydraulic retarder. Parallel hydraulic retarders are installed between the engine and the transmission, using a hydraulic pump to generate resistance and slow down the truck. Tandem hydraulic retarders, on the other hand, are installed behind the transmission and work in conjunction with the vehicle's transmission brake. Both types of hydraulic retarders provide additional braking force to heavy trucks, improving their safety and control on steep slopes.</p></p>
