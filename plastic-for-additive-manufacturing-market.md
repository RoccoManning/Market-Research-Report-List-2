<p><p>Plastic for additive manufacturing refers to the various types of plastic materials that can be used in 3D printing. There are three main market types within this field. Resin-based materials are liquid polymers that solidify when exposed to light, allowing for high precision printing. Powder-based materials use fine plastic particles that are fused together layer by layer using heat or a binding agent. Filament market involves feeding a spooled plastic filament into a printer, which melts and deposits the plastic layer by layer to form a 3D object.</p></p>
