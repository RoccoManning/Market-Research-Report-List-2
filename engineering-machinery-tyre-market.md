<p><p>The engineering machinery tyre market can be categorized into different types based on their design and surface properties. The bar type tyres have a tread pattern with raised bars that provide enhanced traction and stability in rough terrains. Traction type tyres are designed with deep grooves to maximize grip and minimize slippage in muddy or soft terrains. Block type tyres have large, square tread blocks that offer excellent traction on various surfaces. Meanwhile, slick surface tyres have a smooth tread pattern, providing reduced rolling resistance and improved fuel efficiency on paved roads.</p></p>
