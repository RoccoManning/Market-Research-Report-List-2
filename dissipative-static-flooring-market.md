<p><p>Dissipative static flooring refers to flooring materials designed to dissipate static electricity, preventing damage to sensitive electronic equipment. There are three main types of dissipative static flooring: melamine surface, PVC surface, and ceramic surface. Melamine surface flooring is made of a resin-based material and provides a smooth and durable surface. PVC surface flooring uses a layer of polyvinyl chloride to create a static dissipative surface. Ceramic surface flooring is made of ceramic tiles and offers a high level of durability and resistance to chemicals and scratches.</p></p>
