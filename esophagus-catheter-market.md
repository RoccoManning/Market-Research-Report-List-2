<p><p>An esophagus catheter, also known as an esophageal catheter, is a medical device used for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes in the esophagus. Esophagus catheters are commonly used in procedures like esophageal manometry, pH monitoring, and impedance testing to diagnose conditions like gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD), hiatal hernia, and achalasia.</p><p>The current outlook of the esophagus catheter market is moderately positive, with growth expected to continue in the coming years.
