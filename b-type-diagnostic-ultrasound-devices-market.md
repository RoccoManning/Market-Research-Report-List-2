<p><p>B-type diagnostic ultrasound devices are used for non-invasive imaging of various organs and tissues in the body. They come in three types: fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type. Fanshaped scanning devices use a wide-angle transducer to produce a sector-shaped image. Linear scanning devices use a linear transducer to create a rectangular image. Mixed type devices combine both fanshaped and linear scanning techniques to provide a comprehensive view of the target area. These different types cater to the specific needs of healthcare professionals in diagnosing and monitoring medical conditions.</p></p>
