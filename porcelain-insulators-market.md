<p><p>Breakdown type porcelain insulators refer to the type of insulators that are designed to break if a fault occurs in the system. These insulators help protect the equipment by sacrificing themselves and preventing further damage. On the other hand, non-breakdown type porcelain insulators are designed to withstand faults without breaking. They are used in systems where it is crucial to maintain electrical continuity even during faults, thus ensuring uninterrupted power supply. Both types serve different purposes depending on the system requirements and are essential in the porcelain insulators market.</p></p>
