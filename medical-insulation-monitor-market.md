<p><p>A Medical Insulation Monitor (MIM) is a device primarily used to monitor the insulation status of medical electrical equipment and systems to ensure patient safety. MIMs are widely used in healthcare facilities such as hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic centers.</p><p>The growing concern regarding patient safety and the increasing adoption of electrical medical devices are significant factors driving the market growth for medical insulation monitors.
