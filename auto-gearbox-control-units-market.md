<p><p>Auto gearbox control units refer to electronic systems that control the functioning of automatic transmissions in vehicles. The market for these units can be categorized into two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic gearboxes integrate manual operation with automated systems, allowing the driver to shift gears manually or opt for automated shifting. On the other hand, fully automatic gearboxes rely entirely on automated systems that shift gears automatically without any manual input. Both types offer convenience and ease of driving, but differ in the level of control given to the driver.</p></p>
