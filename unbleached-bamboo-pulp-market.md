<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Caustic Soda Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unbleached bamboo pulp market consists of two main types: the sulfate process market and the caustic soda process market. In the sulfate process, bamboo chips are cooked in a solution of sodium hydroxide and sodium sulfide, which breaks down the fibers to produce pulp. On the other hand, the caustic soda process involves boiling bamboo chips in a caustic soda solution to extract the pulp. Both processes offer different advantages and have varying applications in industries such as paper manufacturing and textile production.</p></p>
