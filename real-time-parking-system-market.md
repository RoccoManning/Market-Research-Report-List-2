<p><p>A Real-Time Parking System is a technology-driven solution that provides real-time information about parking space availability in a particular area or facility. This integration not only enhances the efficiency of parking systems but also enables the development of predictive parking solutions.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the adoption of mobile applications for real-time parking.
