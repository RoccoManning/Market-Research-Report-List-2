<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The digital gas meter market consists of two key types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR enables gas consumption readings to be automatically collected and transmitted without human involvement, providing accurate billing and reducing manual errors. On the other hand, AMI includes two-way communication capabilities, allowing bi-directional data exchange between utility companies and consumers. This enables remote monitoring and control of gas usage, facilitating real-time data analysis for efficient energy management. AMI provides additional functionalities and benefits compared to AMR, such as outage detection and demand response.</p></p>
