<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Ice Protection Systems market consists of different types of systems such as pneumatic de-icing boots, electro-thermal, electro-mechanical, TKS ice protection, bleed air, and passive systems. Pneumatic de-icing boots use inflatable rubber boots on the wings and tail surfaces to break and shed ice. Electro-thermal systems employ electric current to heat the surfaces and prevent ice formation. Electro-mechanical systems use mechanical actuators to physically remove ice. TKS ice protection involves a fluid-based anti-icing system. Bleed air systems utilize warm air extracted from the engines to prevent ice formation. Passive systems rely on the aircraft's design and material to avoid ice accumulation.</p></p>
