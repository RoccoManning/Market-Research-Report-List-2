<p><ul><li>Linear Switches</li><li>Tactile Non-Clicky Switches</li><li>Clicky Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical keyboards market can be categorized into three types based on the switches they use. Linear switches are smooth and offer consistent keystrokes without any clicking noise. Tactile non-clicky switches provide a tactile bump while typing, offering feedback without producing a clicking sound. Clicky switches produce an audible click sound with each keystroke, providing both tactile and auditory feedback. These different types cater to the varied preferences of users, allowing them to choose keyboards that suit their typing style and personal preferences.</p></p>
