<p><p>Barrier isolators are advanced containment systems used to manage the risks associated with working with hazardous materials in various industries. Closed type barrier isolators maintain a completely sealed environment, preventing any inward or outward contamination. They are suitable for highly toxic substances. On the other hand, open type barrier isolators have a physical barrier that shields the operator from the process while allowing limited openings for material transfer. They are ideal for handling less hazardous materials and provide some level of protection while enabling straightforward operation.</p></p>
