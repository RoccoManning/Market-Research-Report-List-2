This improves fuel efficiency and power output. Diesel Direct Injection (DDI) is a similar system used in diesel engines, where fuel is directly injected into the combustion chamber as well. Both GDI and DDI systems offer better fuel economy, reduced emissions, and improved engine performance.</p></p>
