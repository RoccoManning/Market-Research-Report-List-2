It is commonly used in construction and demolition applications. On the other hand, Electric Demolition Grapple utilizes electric power for operation, making it more environmentally friendly and quieter. It is often preferred for indoor demolition projects where noise and emissions need to be minimized. Both types offer efficient and effective solutions for handling and removing debris during demolitions.</p></p>
