<p><p>Anti-jam systems aim to prevent or counteract jamming signals that disrupt communication or navigation systems. Nulling systems utilize multiple antennas to nullify jamming signals by creating interference in the opposite phase, cancelling out the interfering signal. Beam steering systems adapt their direction of radiation to avoid jamming signals, ensuring uninterrupted communication or navigation. Civilian systems, designed for non-military applications, provide protection against jamming threats in everyday devices such as smartphones, GPS receivers, or Wi-Fi routers. These systems as a whole contribute to enhancing signal reliability and minimizing disruptions caused by intentional interference.</p></p>
