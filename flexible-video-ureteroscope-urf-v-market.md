<p><p>The flexible video ureteroscope URF V market is divided into three types: CMOS, CCD, and Others. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) are two common imaging sensor technologies used in video ureteroscopes. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption, faster digital readout rates, and improved image quality. CCD sensors, on the other hand, offer better light sensitivity and lower noise levels. The market's "Others" category includes alternative video ureteroscope technologies that are not based on CMOS or CCD sensors.</p></p>
