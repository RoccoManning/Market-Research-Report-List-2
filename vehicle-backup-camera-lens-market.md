<p><p>The vehicle backup camera lens market can be divided into two types: CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras, or charge-coupled device cameras, use a sensor technology that provides high image quality and low noise levels. They are known for their superior performance in low-light conditions. On the other hand, CMOS cameras, or complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor cameras, are more cost-effective and consume less power. Although they may not offer the same image quality as CCD cameras, they are compact and widely used in vehicle backup camera systems.</p></p>
