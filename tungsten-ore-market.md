<p><p>Tungsten ore market is divided into three main types: wolframite, scheelite, and ferberite. Wolframite is a black-brown ore and is the most abundant tungsten source. Scheelite is a white ore and contains a large amount of calcium tungstate, making it an important source as well. Ferberite is a black ore and contains a high percentage of iron. These different types have varying ore compositions and characteristics, which impacts their market value and significance in the tungsten industry.</p></p>
