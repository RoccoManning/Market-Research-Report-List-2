<p><p>The grow medium market consists of various types such as soilless mixes, composts, gravel, inert mediums, and other options. Soilless mixes are composed of various organic materials without any soil content and are widely used for indoor and hydroponic growing. Composts are natural fertilizers made from decomposed organic matter, providing essential nutrients to plants. Gravel is commonly used in hydroponic systems, allowing water to circulate around the plant's roots. Inert mediums like rockwool or perlite provide support and aeration for the plants. Lastly, the "other" category includes less commonly used mediums like coco coir, vermiculite, or sand.</p></p>
