<p><p>Auto wind deflectors come in two main types: in-channel and tape-on. In-channel deflectors are inserted directly into the window channel and provide a sleek and seamless appearance. They also offer better protection against wind and rain intrusion. On the other hand, tape-on deflectors are installed on the outside of the window frame using adhesive tape. They are easier to install and don't require any modifications, but may not provide as snug of a fit. Both types serve the purpose of reducing wind noise, deflecting rain, and improving the overall driving experience.</p></p>
