<p><p>Driver Assistance Transceivers Market includes two major types: Radar Transceivers and DSRC Safety Modem. Radar transceivers use radio waves to detect and track objects on the road, helping drivers with collision avoidance and adaptive cruise control. Meanwhile, DSRC Safety Modems establish short-range wireless communication between vehicles, allowing them to exchange information about their speed, position, and intentions. This enables advanced safety features like cross-traffic alerts and cooperative adaptive cruise control. Both technologies enhance driver assistance systems and contribute to safer and more efficient driving experiences.</p></p>
