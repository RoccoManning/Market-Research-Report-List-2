<p><strong>The Deionized Water System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-bed Deionized Water System</li><li>Mixed-bed Deionized Water System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Deionized Water System market consists of two main types: Two-bed Deionized Water System and Mixed-bed Deionized Water System. </p><p>The Two-bed Deionized Water System involves using two separate resin beds for the removal of positive and negative ions from water. The water passes through the cation and anion resin beds, resulting in moderately pure deionized water.</p><p>On the other hand, the Mixed-bed Deionized Water System combines both positive and negative ion-exchange resins in a single vessel. This process ensures highly pure deionized water by efficiently removing all remaining ions.</p><p>Both systems cater to different needs depending on the desired level of water purity required for specific applications.</p></p>
