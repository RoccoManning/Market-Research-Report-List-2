<p><p>The Automatic Visual Inspection System (AVIS) market can be divided into two main types - Front Inspection and Back Inspection. Front Inspection refers to the analysis and evaluation of the front surface of an object or product using advanced optical technology to detect defects or anomalies. Conversely, Back Inspection involves the examination of the back surface of an object or product to ensure its integrity and identify any potential issues. AVIS technology plays a crucial role in quality control and manufacturing processes by allowing for accurate and efficient detection of flaws or irregularities.</p></p>
