Pure MDI is a type of MDI that contains only one isocyanate group, making it suitable for rigid foam applications. On the other hand, polymeric MDI contains a mixture of MDI isomers with multiple isocyanate groups, making it ideal for flexible foam and elastomer manufacturing. These different market types cater to various industries that require specific properties in their polyurethane products.</p></p>
