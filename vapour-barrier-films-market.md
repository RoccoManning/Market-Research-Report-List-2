<p><p>Vapour barrier films are used in construction to restrict the transmission of moisture through building components. There are different types of vapour barrier films on the market. Sheet films are made of polyethylene and are commonly installed on walls and roofs. Fluid applied films are liquid coatings that are sprayed or rolled onto surfaces to create a vapour barrier. Peel and stick films have an adhesive backing and are easy to install on various surfaces. Laminated vapour barrier films consist of multiple layers for enhanced protection against moisture intrusion.</p></p>
