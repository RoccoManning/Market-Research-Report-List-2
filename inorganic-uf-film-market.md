<p><p>In the inorganic UF (Ultrafiltration) film market, two types- Type I and Type II- can be distinguished. Type I films have a dense structure and high rejection capabilities, making them suitable for various applications including water treatment and purification. On the other hand, Type II films have a loose structure and lower rejection capabilities, making them more suitable for applications where permeability is desired over high rejection. Both types have their specific advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the UF film market.</p></p>
