<p><p>The bunkering service market offers various types of marine fuels for ships. Marine Diesel Fuel (MDO) is a low-sulfur fuel commonly used in smaller vessels. Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO) is the most widely used fuel type, with high sulfur content and lower cost but higher environmental impact.</p></p>
