<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in various applications. The SPF market can be categorized into three types: open cell, closed cell, and others. Open cell SPF consists of cells that are not completely closed, allowing air to fill the spaces within the material. Closed cell SPF, on the other hand, has a cellular structure that is completely closed, preventing air leakage. The "Other" category includes specialized SPF products, such as hybrid systems or high-density SPF, which have unique characteristics or applications.</p></p>
