<p><p>The cable conduits market includes two main types: rigid cable conduit and flexible cable conduit. Rigid cable conduit refers to a rigid structure typically made of metal or plastic that provides strong protection to cables. It is commonly used in applications where cables need to be protected against physical damage or environmental factors. On the other hand, flexible cable conduit offers more versatility, as it is made of flexible materials such as PVC or metal with a corrugated design that allows bending and twisting. It is suitable for installations that require flexibility and ease of routing cables in tight spaces.</p></p>
