<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Gear lubricant market types can be categorized into three main segments - rust & corrosion inhibited, compounded, and extreme pressure lubricants. Rust & corrosion inhibited gear lubricants are formulated to prevent the formation of rust and corrosion on gear surfaces, ensuring their optimal performance and longevity. Compounded gear lubricants contain special additives that enhance their ability to resist oxidation and improve overall gear protection. On the other hand, extreme pressure gear lubricants are designed to withstand high pressure and heavy loads, offering superior protection against wear, pitting, and damage.</p></p>
