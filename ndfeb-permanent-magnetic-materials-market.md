Sintered NdFeB magnets are made by compacting fine NdFeB powder into a desired shape and then sintering them at high temperatures. On the other hand, Bonded NdFeB magnets are manufactured by mixing NdFeB powder with a binder and then pressing or injecting them into the desired shape. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
