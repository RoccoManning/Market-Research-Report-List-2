<p><p>The veterinary infusion pump market includes two main types of pumps - large-volume infusion pumps and syringe infusion pumps. Large-volume infusion pumps are designed for the administration of fluids in larger volumes, such as blood products or intravenous medications. Syringe infusion pumps, on the other hand, are used for more precise and controlled delivery of fluids in smaller volumes, using syringes. Both types of pumps are essential in veterinary clinics and hospitals to ensure accurate and safe administration of fluids to animals in need of medical treatment.</p></p>
