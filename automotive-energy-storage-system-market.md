<p><ul><li>Flywheel</li><li>Batteries</li><li>Super Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive energy storage system market encompasses three main types: flywheels, batteries, and super capacitors. Flywheels store energy in a rotating disc, which can be rapidly charged and discharged. Batteries store energy chemically and provide a steady power supply for longer durations. Super capacitors, on the other hand, store energy electrostatically and deliver quick bursts of power. These energy storage systems are crucial for electric vehicles and hybrid cars as they improve overall vehicle efficiency and enable regenerative braking.</p></p>
