<p><p>Proportional solenoid valves, used in the medical field, come in two types. Direct Acting Proportional Solenoid Valves operate by adjusting the current flow through the coils, allowing for precise control of fluid flow. Pilot Proportional Solenoid Valves use a smaller control servo valve along with a pilot valve, which helps regulate the fluid flow. Both types are essential in medical applications where accurate and controlled fluid flow is required, such as anesthesia delivery, drug infusion systems, and ventilators.</p></p>
