E-coat is an electrically charged coating that provides corrosion protection for metal surfaces. Primer acts as a bonding agent between the substrate and the topcoat, improving adhesion and durability. Clear coat is a transparent top layer that adds gloss and enhances the appearance of the aircraft. Other market segments may include heat-resistant coatings, anti-icing coatings, and specialty coatings tailored for specific aerospace applications. These coatings play a crucial role in protecting aircraft structures and ensuring longevity.</p></p>
