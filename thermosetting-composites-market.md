<p><p>The thermosetting composites market is comprised of different types including short fiber reinforced thermosetting (SFRT), long fiber reinforced thermosetting (LFRT), and glass fiber reinforced thermosetting (GFRT). SFRT composites are made up of short fibers that are randomly dispersed and offer excellent dimensional stability. LFRT composites contain long continuous fibers, providing higher strength and stiffness. GFRT composites are reinforced with glass fibers, offering outstanding mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals and heat. These different types cater to various applications and industries in the thermosetting composites market.</p></p>
