On the other hand, ultrafiltration is a more advanced filtration process that uses a tighter membrane to remove even smaller particles, including viruses and macromolecules. Both types of membrane technology are widely used in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage processing.</p></p>
