<p><p>Nano crystalline cellulose is a material derived from cellulose, a natural polymer found in plants. The market for nano crystalline cellulose is segmented into two types: sulfuric acid hydrolysis and enzymatic hydrolysis. Sulfuric acid hydrolysis involves treating cellulose with sulfuric acid to break it down into nano-sized particles. Enzymatic hydrolysis, on the other hand, uses enzymes to catalyze the breakdown of cellulose into the desired nano crystalline cellulose particles. Both methods offer unique benefits and are used in various industries such as packaging, composites, and biomedical applications.</p></p>
