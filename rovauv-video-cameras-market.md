<p><strong>The ROV/AUV Video Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Sensor Type</li><li>CCD Sensor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ROV/AUV video cameras market includes two main types: CMOS sensor type and CCD sensor type. CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors are more popular due to their lower power consumption, smaller size, and faster readout speeds. They offer good image quality for simple underwater imaging tasks. On the other hand, CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors provide higher image quality, better light sensitivity, and dynamic range, making them suitable for more sophisticated underwater applications where accurate and detailed imaging is required. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the ROV/AUV industry.</p></p>
