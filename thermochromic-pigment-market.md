<p><p>The Thermochromic Pigment Market consists of two main types: Reversible Thermochromic Pigment and Irreversible Thermochromic Pigment. Reversible Thermochromic Pigment refers to a pigment that changes color temporarily based on temperature variations, returning to its original color once the temperature returns to its normal state. On the other hand, Irreversible Thermochromic Pigment changes color permanently when exposed to specific temperatures, meaning it will not revert back to its original color even if the temperature changes again. Both types offer unique color-changing properties and find applications in various industries such as textiles, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
