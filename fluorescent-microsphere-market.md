<p><p>The fluorescent microsphere market includes two main types: hollow microspheres and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are composed of a thin shell with a hollow interior, providing exceptional buoyancy and reduced density. These microspheres are commonly used in medical and biological applications, as well as in diagnostics and bioimaging techniques. On the other hand, solid microspheres are made of a dense material throughout, making them more durable and resistant to crushing. Solid microspheres find applications in a wide range of industries, including paints, coatings, and composites for their unique properties.</p></p>
