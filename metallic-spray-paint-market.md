<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic spray paint market can be classified into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic spray paints contain solvents as the main component, which evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a metallic-colored finish. These paints provide excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various surfaces. On the other hand, water-based metallic spray paints are formulated with water as the primary solvent. They are more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up than solvent-based paints, while still offering a vibrant metallic appearance.</p></p>
