<p><ul><li>Open-loop Current Sensor</li><li>Closed loop Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current sensing chips are key components used in various applications to measure and monitor electric current flow. Open-loop current sensors provide a non-contact method to measure current based on the magnetic field generated by the current. These sensors are highly accurate and suitable for high current applications. On the other hand, closed-loop current sensors utilize a feedback loop to compensate for inaccuracies, ensuring precise measurements. These sensors offer superior isolation and are commonly used in low current applications or when high accuracy is required.</p></p>
