<p><strong>The Chemical Foam Blowing Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endothermic</li><li>Exothermic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical foam blowing agents are used in various industries for producing foam materials. There are two main types of chemical foam blowing agents: endothermic and exothermic. </p><p>Endothermic agents absorb heat during their decomposition, producing gas that expands and forms foam. They are commonly used in applications where heat-sensitive materials are involved, as they help to prevent thermal damage.</p><p>Exothermic agents release heat during their decomposition, producing gas that expands and forms foam. They are often employed in applications requiring high temperatures, as they can help to improve cell structure and foam expansion.</p><p>Both types of blowing agents play crucial roles in the production of foam materials, but their specific characteristics determine their suitability for different applications.</p></p>
