<p><p>Concrete admixtures are chemicals added to concrete during the mixing process to enhance its properties. Superplasticizers improve workability and allow for the reduction of water content. Normal plasticizers also aid in workability but to a lesser extent. Accelerating agents shorten the setting time, while retarding agents delay it. Air-entraining agents create small air bubbles for increased resistance to freeze-thaw cycles. Waterproofing agents reduce permeability, while others include corrosion inhibitors and coloring agents. These admixtures offer various benefits depending on the desired outcome of the concrete mixture.</p></p>
