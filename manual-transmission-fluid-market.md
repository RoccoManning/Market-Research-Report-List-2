<p><p>The manual transmission fluid (MTF) market can be classified into three types: Mineral MTF, Synthetic MTF, and Semi-Synthetic MTF. Mineral MTF is made from refined crude oil and offers basic lubrication and protection for manual transmissions. Synthetic MTF is manufactured using chemical processes, providing superior performance and protection in extreme conditions. Semi-Synthetic MTF is a blend of mineral and synthetic oils, offering a balance between cost-effectiveness and enhanced performance. These different types cater to various needs and preferences of users in the manual transmission fluid market.</p></p>
