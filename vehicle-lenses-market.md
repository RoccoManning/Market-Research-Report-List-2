<p><p>The vehicle lenses market can be classified into two major types: external lenses and interior lenses. External lenses refer to the lenses used on the exterior parts of a vehicle, such as headlights, taillights, and turn signals. These lenses are designed to provide visibility and safety by directing and amplifying light. On the other hand, interior lenses are used inside the vehicle, including dashboard displays, infotainment screens, and instrument clusters. These lenses enhance the visual experience, provide essential information, and improve the overall aesthetics of the vehicle's interior.</p></p>
