<p><p>Color tunable products refer to lighting solutions that allow users to adjust the color temperature or the color appearance of the light emitted. In the market, there are several types available. Incandescent and halogen lights produce light by heating a filament, while fluorescent lights utilize electricity to excite mercury vapor and produce ultraviolet light. LED lights use semiconductors to emit light of various colors, providing energy-efficient and long-lasting options. HID lights, on the other hand, produce light by passing an electric arc through ionized gas. These products offer consumers flexibility in creating different lighting atmospheres.</p></p>
