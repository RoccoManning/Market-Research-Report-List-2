<p><ul><li>Cobalt-Base Wear-Resistant Alloys</li><li>Cobalt-Base High-Temperature Alloys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cobalt alloys are categorized into two main types, namely cobalt-base wear-resistant alloys and cobalt-base high-temperature alloys. Cobalt-base wear-resistant alloys are specially designed to withstand high levels of wear and abrasion, making them ideal for applications such as cutting tools, mining equipment, and turbine blades. On the other hand, cobalt-base high-temperature alloys possess excellent heat resistance and are used in industries that require materials capable of maintaining strength and stability at elevated temperatures, such as aerospace, gas turbines, and nuclear reactors.</p></p>
