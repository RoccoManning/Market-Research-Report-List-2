<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The PE Beverage Bottle Caps market consists of two main types: injection caps and compression mould caps. Injection caps are made by injecting melted polyethylene into a mold cavity, resulting in a solid cap. These caps are known for their high durability and tight sealing properties. On the other hand, compression mould caps are created by compressing the heated polyethylene material into a pre-formed shape. These caps are more lightweight and cost-effective compared to injection caps, making them an attractive option for certain beverage manufacturers.</p></p>
