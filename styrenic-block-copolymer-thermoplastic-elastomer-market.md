<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymer Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPEs) are a type of material that combines the properties of rubber and plastics. They are widely used in various industries for their excellent elasticity, flexibility, and durability. There are three main types of Styrenic Block Copolymer TPEs – SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene), and SIS (Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene). SBS TPEs have balanced properties and are commonly used in shoe soles and adhesives. SEBS TPEs offer enhanced oil resistance and are commonly used in automotive components and medical devices. SIS TPEs provide excellent tackiness and are used in various applications such as labels, tapes, and consumer goods.</p></p>
