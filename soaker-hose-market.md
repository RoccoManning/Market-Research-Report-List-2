<p><p>Soaker hoses are a type of irrigation system used for watering plants and gardens efficiently. The market offers two main types: flat soaker hoses and round soaker hoses. Flat soaker hoses are thin and flat, making them ideal for use in garden beds as they can be easily positioned and hidden under mulch. Round soaker hoses, on the other hand, have a circular shape and are commonly used for larger areas or long straight rows. Both types provide a slow, steady release of water directly to the roots, promoting healthy plant growth.</p></p>
