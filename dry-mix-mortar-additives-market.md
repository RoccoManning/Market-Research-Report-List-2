<p><ul><li>Redispersible Polymer Powder</li><li>Plasticizers</li><li>Defoamers</li><li>Cellulose Ether</li><li>Air Entraining Agents</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry mix mortar additives are substances added to mortar mixtures to enhance their performance and properties. One type is redispersible polymer powder, which improves mortar flexibility and adhesion. Plasticizers are additives that improve mortar workability and reduce water content. Defoamers eliminate foam formation during mixing, enhancing mortar stability. Cellulose ethers increase mortar water retention and improve workability. Air entraining agents improve mortar freeze-thaw resistance by incorporating tiny air bubbles. Finally, other additives may include accelerators, retarders, and anti-crack agents, which provide specific benefits to the mortar mixture.</p></p>
