<p><p>The Automotive EGR Gas Temperature Sensor market consists of two types: Resistance Type and Thermocouples Type sensors. Resistance Type sensors measure temperature by detecting changes in electrical resistance. They are commonly used in automotive applications due to their accuracy and cost-effectiveness. Thermocouples Type sensors work on the principle of the Seebeck effect, generating an electric current when there is a temperature difference between the junctions. They offer high temperature range and durability, making them suitable for harsh automotive environments. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling exhaust gas temperature in vehicles.</p></p>
