<p><strong>The Automotive Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive starter motors are classified into two types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starter motors are controlled by a switch that directly connects the battery to the starter motor. They are typically found in older vehicles. On the other hand, electromagnetic control starter motors use a solenoid to control the current flow from the battery to the starter motor. This type is commonly found in modern vehicles and offers more efficient and reliable starting capabilities.</p></p>
