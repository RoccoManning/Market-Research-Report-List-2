There are two types of cleanroom constant climate chambers:</p><p>1. Box Type: These chambers are designed like a box and typically offer a smaller space for maintaining constant climate conditions. They are suitable for laboratories or small-scale applications where a limited number of products or samples need to be tested or stored under specific environmental conditions.</p><p>2. Enter Type: These chambers have a larger capacity and are designed for accommodating larger quantities of products or samples. They often have multiple access points, allowing for easy entry and retrieval of items. Enter type chambers are commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics where a larger number of products require constant climate control.</p><p>Both box type and enter type cleanroom constant climate chambers provide a reliable and precise environment for applications that demand specific temperature, humidity, and cleanliness conditions.</p></p>
