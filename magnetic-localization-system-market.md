<p><p>The Magnetic Localization System Market can be categorized into two types: Rigid Magnetic Localization System and Flexible Magnetic Localization System. The Rigid Magnetic Localization System refers to a system that uses rigid magnetic sensors to track and locate objects or devices. On the other hand, the Flexible Magnetic Localization System adopts flexible magnetic sensors for tracking and locating purposes. Both systems provide accurate and reliable positioning information but differ in terms of the type of magnetic sensors used.</p></p>
