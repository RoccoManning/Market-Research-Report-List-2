<p><p>The Packaging Metallized Film market includes various types of films such as PET Film, CPP Film, and BOPP Film. PET Film, also known as Polyester film, is a highly durable and heat-resistant material commonly used for packaging applications. CPP Film, or Cast Polypropylene film, is known for its excellent chemical resistance and transparency. BOPP Film, or Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene film, is widely used for flexible packaging due to its high tensile strength and moisture barrier properties. These films play a crucial role in enhancing the shelf life and protection of various products during transportation and storage.</p></p>
