<p><p>Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF) is a technology used to enhance the brightness and energy efficiency of display devices, such as LCD screens. There are different types of BEF available in the market. Normal prism is a basic type that improves light output. Multi-function prism provides additional optical functionalities, like diffusion and reflection. Micro Lens Film incorporates tiny lenses to increase brightness and clarity. DBEF (Dual Brightness Enhancement Film) improves both brightness and viewing angles. These different market types of BEF cater to various needs in the display industry, ensuring better visual experiences and energy savings.</p></p>
